About Us

BitBrush provides virtual reality visualization services for existing and new real estate development projects. BitBrush is serving real estate developers, architects, and civil firms since 1995. We provide services such as: 3D renditions, raster to vector conversion, architectural and structural drafting, steel detailing for the AEC community, as well as GIS mapping and data analysis for GIS discipline. We also provide professional architectural rendering, 3D modeling, and animation walk-through for the architectural community.

BitBrush is committed to :-

  • Fast turnaround of products
  • Competitive pricing
  • Rigorous quality control
  • Customized services
  • Prompt responses to project queries
  • Effective communication with clients

BitBrush has completed 4000 3D animation projects. Clients love us for our responsiveness, flexibility, timeliness, and competitive pricing. We would love to partner with your business to make your projects come to life.
